    What will the implementation process of our Hotel Revenue Management Services be like?

    1. Analysis and diagnosis of the situation

    In order to achieve improvements, we need to meticulously examine the starting point, from the rooms, facilities and experiences offered by the hotel, to each of the details; the prices, demand, characteristics of the clients, competitors, hotel marketing strategies, website of the establishment... Only by doing this we'll be able to take the next step forward and specify what we want to improve and how we are going to do it.

    What is the real starting point?

    2. Definition of objectives and strategies

    Now that we are clear about our starting point, we are going to focus on improving and making the situation as profitable as possible. We will establish the objectives that we want to achieve and define the hotel marketing strategies that we are going to apply in order to attain a balanced position between the key factors in the sale process: product, client, time and price. The theory is in place, but before putting it into practice, we need to disseminate the new developments.

    What are the business goals and how are we going to achieve them?

    3. Involve the entire team

    Each of the members of your team need to be aligned under the same guidelines, those of Revenue Management. By doing this, we will achieve the necessary cohesion in order to successfully develop the strategies agreed and optimise every sale. We will all focus our work on safeguarding the interests of the project, the different departments will prepare in order to improve their procedures in a coordinated way. It is time to take notice, just before taking action.

    All the participants will be imbued with this working philosophy

    4. Implementation

    With the objectives and strategies of your hotel well-defined, and the team trained and ready, the most powerful phase of this process begins, that of implementing marketing strategies and beginning to obtain results. Revenue Management involves constant activity, flexibility and adapting to a changing tourist market, a capacity for analysis and ongoing assessment of processes and results. Immersion in this culture will be highly beneficial for your company.
