    What steps do we take in order to optimise the management of hotel investments?

    1. Analysis of the situation

    First of all, we need to obtain a clear and objective view of the initial situation. Whether dealing with a new project that is still an idea on paper, land or hotels on sale, a future refurbishment or extension...or any other situation that involves an investment, we will carry out a meticulous study of all the conditioning factors, both economic and financial and those relating to legal matters.

    We need to know our starting point

    2. Data collection

    We are going to consider all the data that may be important: economic and financial situation of the assets, business plan for the possible purchase, future prospects for the business, situation of the market at that specific time... With all of that, we will obtain the figures that we need and that will enable us to determine the value of the asset, the costs of the project and, ultimately, the final price of the operation.

    We need accurate and up-to-date information

    3. Assessment of options

    Now that we are clear about the actual situation that we find ourselves in, it is time to assess the implications and consequences of deciding one way or another. Studying the data will enable us to foresee the effects of the transaction and from there, we will deduct if it is appropriate to go ahead or if, on the contrary, it is preferable to cancel the process and reconsider the strategy.

    We study the possible effects and results

    4. Decision making

    Armed with the assurance of having the right information and data, and clear about what deciding one way or another will mean, the moment of truth has arrived. As consultants who are experts in the management of hotel investments, we are going to offer you the optimum solution for the situation that you present to us, seeking maximum profitability and based on the real economic potential of the asset.
